IP Commerce

Position: Marketing Engineer
Duration: November 2010 - April 2013
Industry: Financial Services
Company Size: 11 - 50 employees

At IP Commerce, I combined technical and marketing expertise to support product marketing initiatives for a payment processing software company. My role involved bridging the gap between technical product capabilities and market-facing messaging to effectively communicate value propositions to target audiences.

IP Commerce was an innovative payment processing software company that provided a platform for developers to integrate payment processing capabilities into their applications. The company's technology enabled software developers to easily connect to multiple payment processors through a single API, simplifying the complex payment ecosystem.

As a Marketing Engineer, I worked at the intersection of product development and marketing, translating technical features into compelling benefits and value propositions. I collaborated closely with both engineering and marketing teams to ensure accurate and effective communication of product capabilities to the market.

Key Achievements

  • Developed technical marketing materials including white papers, case studies, and product documentation that effectively communicated complex payment processing concepts to non-technical audiences
  • Created and maintained developer-focused content including API documentation, code samples, and integration guides that improved developer onboarding experience
  • Collaborated with product management to define product positioning and messaging based on market needs and technical capabilities
  • Supported sales team with technical expertise during customer meetings and product demonstrations
  • Conducted competitive analysis to identify market opportunities and inform product development priorities
  • Managed the company's developer portal, ensuring up-to-date and accurate technical information
  • Developed and delivered technical presentations at industry events and developer conferences
  • Contributed to product requirements by providing market feedback and customer insights to the product development team
