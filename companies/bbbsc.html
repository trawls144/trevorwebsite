Big Brothers Big Sisters of Colorado

Position: Fund Development Vista
Duration: June 2009 - September 2010
Industry: Non-profit Organization Management
Company Size: 51 - 200 employees

At Big Brothers Big Sisters of Colorado, I served as an AmeriCorps VISTA member supporting fund development initiatives. This role was particularly significant as it marked the beginning of my professional career after graduating from Central Michigan University with a degree in Finance during the 2008 recession, when finance job opportunities were scarce.

Big Brothers Big Sisters of Colorado is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating and supporting one-to-one mentoring relationships between adult volunteers ("Bigs") and children ("Littles"), helping children realize their potential and build their futures. The organization serves thousands of children across Colorado through various mentoring programs.

As a Fund Development VISTA, I was part of the AmeriCorps VISTA (Volunteers in Service to America) program, which is designed to fight poverty in America. My role focused on building capacity for the organization's fundraising efforts, developing sustainable funding sources, and supporting various development initiatives to ensure the organization could continue its important work in the community.

Key Achievements

  • Developed and implemented a donor database management system that improved tracking and reporting of donor information
  • Created fundraising materials and grant proposals that contributed to securing over $100,000 in funding
  • Coordinated volunteer committees for special events, resulting in increased attendance and fundraising success
  • Researched and identified new funding opportunities, expanding the organization's potential donor base
  • Assisted in planning and executing fundraising events that engaged community members and raised awareness about the organization's mission
  • Developed marketing materials to communicate the impact of the organization's programs to potential donors
  • Collaborated with staff across departments to align fundraising efforts with program needs and organizational goals
  • Implemented data analysis processes to measure the effectiveness of fundraising initiatives and inform future strategies
